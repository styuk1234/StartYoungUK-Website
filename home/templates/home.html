Our Aim

To work alongside local schools and communities, supporting young people and families in achieving their full potential as valued members of the community. We engage with children from 5-18 years, supporting them and their families in education, securing links between school and a career. We do this with a range of activities for younger children, whilst arranging apprenticeships, sponsorships and work experience for the young adults. We enable our children to feel valued and our buddy system forges lifelong resilience and inspiration.

If you would like to support, volunteer, donate, or simply just find out more about StartYoungUK and what we do, then please Contact Us or Sign Up

Our Ethos

To establish links with local schools and business to effect change in children’s lives. To have a wide range of people engage with the children and instil hope, value and aspiration in each child. To demonstrate a good work ethic and resilience and be a positive influence in the lives of children.

Ensure that everyone we come into contact with feels valued and that together, we can overcome adversity.
